Our Story

Passion-Driven. Kerala-Rooted.

Zakk Events & Decorations is a Beemapally-based event studio dedicated to making every celebration beautiful — no matter the budget. From the intricate patterns of bridal mehendi to grand wedding décor, we bring Kerala's vibrant traditions together with modern elegance.

Our team handles everything under one roof: decorations, bridal styling, catering, photography, light & sound — so you can relax and enjoy your special day.
