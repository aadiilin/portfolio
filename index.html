Our Story

Passion-Driven. Kerala-Rooted.

Based in Beemapally, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, Zakk Events & Decorations is a premier event decoration studio dedicated to making every celebration beautiful — no matter the budget. From the intricate patterns of bridal mehendi to grand wedding décor, we bring Kerala's vibrant traditions together with modern elegance. We serve clients across TVM, Kollam, Ernakulam and all of Kerala with affordable luxury decorations.

Our team handles everything under one roof: decorations, bridal styling, catering, photography, light & sound — so you can relax and enjoy your special day.
